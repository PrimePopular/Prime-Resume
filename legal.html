Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2025

Short version: We collect nothing. Your resume data never leaves your device. We have no servers storing your personal information.

1. What We Collect

Prime Resume collects no personal data. We do not collect, store, transmit, or process any information you enter into the resume builder.

All data you enter — including your name, contact details, work history, education, and skills — is stored exclusively in your browser's local storage on your own device.

2. Local Storage

Prime Resume uses your browser's local storage to save your resume progress between sessions. This data:

  • Never leaves your device
  • Is never transmitted to any server
  • Can be deleted by you at any time using the "Clear All" button
  • Is automatically removed when you clear your browser data

3. No Tracking

Prime Resume does not use:

  • Analytics or tracking pixels
  • Cookies for tracking purposes
  • Third-party advertising services
  • User behavior monitoring tools
  • Session recording software

4. Premium Activation

When you activate a premium code, the code and activation date are stored in your browser's local storage only. This information is not transmitted to us or any third party.

5. Exports

When you export your resume as PDF, the file is generated entirely within your browser. The exported file is saved directly to your device and is never transmitted through our systems.

6. Children's Privacy

Prime Resume does not knowingly collect any information from children under the age of 13. Since we collect no information at all, this applies universally.

7. Changes to This Policy

If we update this policy, the new version will be posted on this page with an updated date. Continued use of the service after changes constitutes acceptance of the new policy.

8. Contact

For privacy-related questions, please contact us through the support channel provided at the time of your premium purchase.
