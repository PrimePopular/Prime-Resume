Refund Policy

Last updated:31/12/2025

At Prime Resume, customer satisfaction is important to us. This Refund Policy explains when refunds may be issued for purchases made through our platform.

Eligibility for Refunds

Refund requests may be considered under the following conditions:

  • The request is made within 14 days of the original purchase date.
  • The purchase was made directly through our website.
  • The request complies with Paddle’s Buyer Terms, as Paddle acts as our Merchant of Record.

Because Prime Resume provides instant access to digital services, refunds are not guaranteed once the service has been accessed or used. All requests are reviewed on a case-by-case basis.

Non-Refundable Circumstances

Refunds may be declined in the following situations:

  • Requests made after 14 days from the purchase date.
  • Extensive use of premium features prior to the refund request.
  • Abuse of the refund policy or repeated refund requests.
  • Change of mind after successful delivery of the digital service.
