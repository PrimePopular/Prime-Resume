Questions

How does the 40-day premium work? ▼
You pay once and get 40 days of full premium access. After 40 days it reverts to the free plan automatically. Your resume data is never deleted — only the premium features lock again.
Is my data really private? ▼
Yes. Everything runs in your browser. We have no server that stores your resume. Your data lives only on your device in local storage. You can delete it anytime by clicking "Clear All" in the builder.
What happens if I clear my browser data? ▼
Your resume data and premium status are stored in your browser's local storage. If you clear browser data, both will be reset. We recommend exporting your resume as PDF before clearing browser data.
Can I get a refund? ▼
Yes. If you are unsatisfied within 7 days of purchase, contact us for a full refund. See our refund policy for full details.
Do I need an account? ▼
No account needed for anything — free or premium. Premium is activated with a one-time code stored locally on your device.
