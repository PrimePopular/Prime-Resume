The honest story
I did this because I am broke.
And I am trying to do something about it.

I'm a 21 year old CS student at Godfrey Okoye University, Enugu, Nigeria. No fancy background. No startup money. Just a laptop, a dream, and a confirmed amount of late nights.

When I started building My Prime Resume, I wasn't thinking about privacy or ATS systems. I was thinking — can I build something people actually need? Something that proves I can create, not just consume?

Turns out, the resume builder space is filled with tools that charge you $190, take your personal data, and make you wait 5 days for something you could do yourself in 5 minutes. I won't say that bothered me — but I saw an opportunity. So I fixed it.

I have a team — but this? This is just me. I saw a gap. I decided to fill it. Every line of code in this tool was written with that exact intention.

The vision
I want this to feel like your personal career companion.

Not just a builder. Not just a PDF exporter. A tool that actually walks with you through your job search — private, fast, and genuinely useful.

When My Prime Resume has every feature a job seeker truly needs — and I need your help to confirm it works — that's when I stop adding.

No bloat. No features for the sake of features. Just the best possible tool, refined until it's perfect. Or close enough.

That's the goal. A companion you can trust. One that never locks you out and never pretends to be more than it is.
