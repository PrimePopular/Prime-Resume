## Voice Builder (voice.html)

Guided 6-step voice resume flow:

1. **Name & Title** — "Say your full name and your current or desired job title."
2. **Contact Info** — "Say your email address, phone number, and city or country."
3. **Professional Summary** — "Describe yourself professionally in 2–3 sentences."
4. **Work Experience** — "Describe your work experience. Company, role, dates, key achievements."
5. **Education** — "State your degree, institution, and graduation year."
6. **Skills** — "List your key skills and any certifications or achievements."

Each step shows: instruction, example, live transcript, mic button, back/skip/next navigation.  
Audio quality note displayed on every step.  
One Groq API call at the end to structure all transcripts into resume fields.  
Redirects to builder.html with fields pre-filled.

Requires Chrome or Chromium-based browser (Web Speech API).  
1 free use per 24 hours. Premium: 5 uses per day.

## Legal (legal.html)

### Privacy Policy
My Prime Resume does not collect, store, or transmit personal data to any server. All resume data is stored exclusively in the user's browser localStorage. When using AI features, only the relevant text (e.g. your summary or skills) is sent to the AI backend for processing — no personally identifiable information is logged or stored server-side.

## Technical Information (for AI/developers)

**Live URLs:**
- Frontend: https://primepopular.github.io/Prime-Resume/
- Backend: https://prime-resume-backend.vercel.app

**Backend AI Endpoints:**
- `POST /ai/job-match` — Resume vs job description match analysis
- `POST /ai/cover-letter` — Smart cover letter generation
- `POST /ai/improve-summary` — Summary improvement
- `POST /ai/improve-achievement` — Bullet point rewriting
- `POST /ai/generate-summary` — Summary generation from profile data
- `POST /ai/quick-resume` — Structures voice transcript or uploaded text into resume fields

**AI Models:**
- Primary: Groq `llama-3.1-8b-instant`
- Fallback: Google Gemini `gemini-2.0-flash`

**Key Files:**
- `js/data.js` — localStorage data management
- `js/preview.js` — Live resume preview rendering (all 8 templates)
- `js/export.js` — PDF export and feedback popup
- `js/checker.js` — ATS checker, resume score, AI job match
- `js/premium.js` — Premium key validation and activation
- `js/trialmanager.js` — 24h trial system with tamper-resistant localStorage
- `js/voice.js` — Web Speech API guided voice flow
- `css/templates.css` — All 8 template styles
